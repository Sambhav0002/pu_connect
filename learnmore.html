Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join PU Connect?
PU Connect is open to all currently enrolled university students. Faculty members can participate as advisors or moderators.
Is there a cost to use PU Connect?
No, PU Connect is completely free for all university students as part of our commitment to enhancing the student experience.
How are mentors vetted?
All mentors must verify their student status and demonstrate expertise in their chosen mentorship areas through academic records or portfolio review.
Can I be both a mentor and mentee?
Absolutely! Many students mentor in areas they're strong in while seeking mentorship in subjects they want to improve.
How often should mentorship sessions occur?
This varies by pair, but we recommend meeting at least bi-weekly. The platform helps you schedule sessions that work for both parties.
